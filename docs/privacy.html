Privacy Policy

How we handle information

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Krishna Enterprises, operating under the Filymore brand, may collect, use, protect, and disclose information in connection with Filymore AI websites, services, and developer integrations.

Information We May Collect

We may collect business contact information, account identifiers, technical logs, usage information, and information provided through authorized integrations. When using Amazon developer services or APIs, we access only the information authorized by the account owner and required for the stated business purpose.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to provide services, operate integrations, monitor performance, improve workflows, maintain security, respond to inquiries, and comply with applicable legal or platform requirements.

Amazon API Data

Data accessed through Amazon APIs is used only for authorized business functions such as advertising profile access, reporting, automation, and operational analytics. We do not sell Amazon API data.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Credentials and tokens must be stored securely and are not published in public repositories.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, platform providers, or authorities when necessary to operate services, meet legal obligations, or protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for business, operational, legal, security, and compliance purposes. Retention periods may vary based on the nature of the information and applicable requirements.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information where applicable. Integration access may also be revoked through the relevant platform or account authorization settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@filymore.com.
